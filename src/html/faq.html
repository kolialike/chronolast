Buying watches

The offer comes from abroad. What should I consider when buying and what fees apply?

For offers available via Trusted Checkout or Buy It Now, you can order the item by clicking on the green Make a purchase request button or Buy It Now button. You can ask the seller general questions about the offer by clicking on the Questions about this watch?

For all other offers, contact the seller by clicking on the Contact this seller now button.

The seller will receive your message as an e-mail. We have no way of knowing when the dealer will read or answer your message. Alternatively, you can call the dealer. The hone number is listed on the offer page under the Seller information box.
